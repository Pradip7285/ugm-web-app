About Us

Leading Underground Mining Innovation

Headquartered in Kolkata, UGM Mining is a premier underground coal mining company helmed by industry veterans with combined expertise spanning over two centuries. We merge rich experience with forward-looking innovation to transform underground mining from an art to a science.

Our Vision

To lead India in sustainable coal mining, recognized for safety, environmental stewardship, and continuous improvement while powering the nation's development.

Our Mission

To responsibly extract high-quality coal through innovative technologies and operational excellence, ensuring well-being for our workforce, communities, and environment.

"Our commitment to innovation, safety, and environmental stewardship has earned us recognition as one of India's most sustainable mining companies."
